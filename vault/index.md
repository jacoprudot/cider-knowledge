# Cider Institute — Knowledge Library

Welcome to the Cider Institute's AI-powered knowledge library. This vault contains structured knowledge extracted from the official course materials of the [Cider Institute of North America](https://www.ciderinstitute.com/).

## Topics

### 🍏 [Fermentation](/vault/fermentation/)
Fruit selection, harvesting, milling & pressing, juice composition, fermentation management, SO₂ usage, racking, maturation, and malolactic fermentation.
*Source: Peter Mitchell, Foundation Textbook (2021)*

### 🔬 [Lab Testing](/vault/lab-testing/)
Laboratory setup, safety, analytical methods: soluble solids, pH, titratable acidity, free & total SO₂, volatile acidity, alcohol determination, residual sugar, microbiological testing.
*Source: Steven Trussler, Essential Laboratory Testing Manual*

### 👃 [Sensory Analysis](/vault/sensory-analysis/)
The five senses in cider evaluation, aroma and taste profiles, sensory testing methods, fault identification.
*Source: Bri Valliere, Essential Sensory Analysis Course (WSU)*

### 🏭 [Facility Operations](/vault/facility-operations/)
Cidery equipment guide, facility planning (100K bottles/year), GMP, safety & sanitation, SOP templates, production scheduling.
*Source: Peter Mitchell, Facility Planning Toolkit*

### 🍐 [Perry Production](/vault/perry-production/)
Perry pear selection, processing differences from cider, sorbitol considerations, MLF avoidance.
*Source: Peter Mitchell, Foundation Textbook § Perry Production*

### 🧪 [Aroma Chemistry](/vault/aroma-chemistry/)
Esters, higher alcohols, volatile phenolics, sensory impact, production practices affecting aroma.
*Source: CiderCon 2026 Aroma Chemistry Workshop*

## About the Cider Institute

The Cider Institute of North America is a non-profit organization made up of passionate cider industry professionals and educators with a mission to create a quality-driven and sustainable cider industry through education and research.
