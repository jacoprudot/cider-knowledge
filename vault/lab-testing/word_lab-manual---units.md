## Units


There are many units of measurement used in different industries and different countries. To minimize confusion and ensure consistency across disciplines an International System of Units (SI units) has been established and will be used in this manual. Each parameter has a base unit, like gram or mole, which can be modified depending upon the size of what is being measured. This system is based upon intervals of 10. For example, a centimeter is one-tenth of decimeter which in turn is one tenth of a meter, which is one tenth of a dekameter, which is one tenth of a hectometer, which is one-tenth of a kilometer. This makes a centimeter 1/100000th of a kilometer.
