## Using a Microscope


Place the slide on the stage and secure with the arm.

Set the diaphragm to the most open setting and secure the 4x objective lens into the viewing position.

Adjust the stage to its highest position..

Look through the ocular lenses and adjust the gross and fine focus knobs until you can view the sample. Only turn the focus knobs in the direction that lowers the stage. If you must raise the stage for focusing, stop looking though the ocular lenses and watch that the stage does not impact the objective lens.

To view your sample at a higher magnification, rotate the appropriate objective lens into position and adjust the diaphragm to an appropriate amount of light. Only fine focusing should be necessary. Avoid raising the stage.
