Compounds present in apple juice that persist unchanged into cider.
Secondary
Compounds formed as a result of alcoholic or
malolactic fermentation due to either
(i) Normal metabolism of sugars, amino

(ii) Transformation of apple-specific
precursors
Tertiary
Compounds formed during wine storage, for example, as a result of
(i) Extraction from oak
(ii) Microbial spoilage or chemical tainting
(iii) Abiotic transformation of precursor
compounds in cider
